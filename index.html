About Me

Hello and welcome to my portfolio. My name is Dan Lyons and my background is in mechanical engineering. I began my engineering career in the hydro turbine industry at Voith Hydro, where I stayed for the first 9 years in CFD and spent my last year in Structural Analysis. My third job was as a Stress Analyst for Teledyne Energy Systems.

I have an interest in coding and have written code in Perl, TCL, LINUX and FORTRAN and created macros for processing CFD runs while at Voith. My interest in coding is the main reason I am taking this path into web development and I am very excited for the journey ahead.
